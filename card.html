Pikachu

Pikachu can store electricity in its cheeks and release it in lightning

  • Type: Eletric
  • Species: Mouse Pokémon
  • Height: 1′4″ (0.41m)
  • Weight: 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)

Pikachu comes across something new, it blasts it with a jolt of electricity. If you come across a blackened berry, it's evidence that this Pokémon mistook the intensity of its charge.
